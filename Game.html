How to play Mech arena

Download the Game: Install Mech Arena from your app store (Google Play or App Store).

>

Select a Mech: Choose a mech from your collection, each with unique abilities and weapons.

>

Upgrade Your Gear: Use credits and rewards to upgrade mechs and weapons for better performance.

">

Choose a Mode: Play game modes like Control Point Capture, Team Deathmatch, or Free-for-All.

Use the joystick on the left to move. Aim and shoot with the controls on the right.

>

Objective: Complete match objectives, such as capturing points or defeating enemies, to win.

>

Earn Rewards: Win battles to earn credits, experience points, and gear.

">
